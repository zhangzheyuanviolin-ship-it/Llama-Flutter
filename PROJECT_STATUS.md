##  Research Summary

Based on extensive research comparing ChatGPT, Gemini, and Kimi AI responses:

### Key Decisions Made

1. **Pigeon-First Approach** 
   - Simpler than FFI
   - Type-safe
   - Built-in EventChannel support
   - Good enough performance for token streaming

2. **Foreground Service** 
   - More appropriate than WorkManager for real-time inference
   - Predictable behavior
   - Requires \FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE\ permission

3. **Android 15 Compliance** 
   - 16KB page size enforced in build
   - NDK r27+ requirement
   - Deadline: Nov 1, 2025 for new apps

4. **MIT License** 
   - Commercial-friendly
   - Unlike fllama (GPLv2)
   - Allows proprietary use
